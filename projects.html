My Projects

Product Placement for Seed Production

Each year, seed production companies are producing milions units of seeds, allocating the seed to production locations while satisfying the capacity and agronomic constraints is a sensitive and difficult task. We have developed an optimized solution for seed placement in which while satisfying all the constraints, the total production cost is minimized!

Post-Harvest Processing Optimal Planning for Seed Production and Supply Chain

After the harvest seeds are going through different processing steps, allocation and scheduling of all the products to different processing facilities based on the available capacity and due dates is a complicated problem. We developed an optimization solution to find the best solution to process all the seeds in a timely manner and lowest cost possible!

Order fulfillment Optimization

Each year we plant many experiments in different locations, each experiment has several different type of seeds with specific treatments! Overall we have several thousands orders with milions of items in each order. In this project we provided a simulation tool for our custome-made sortation system to minimize the order fulfillment.

What else?

Seed Advisory Optimization

Product placement in farmers fields. Based on multiple features such as field size, field locations, historical information, weather and seed yield and seed availability, we prescribe the best seed for our customers.

Distribution Planning

After post-harvest processing, seeds are distributed to different distribution centers and agencies. We help our logistic team to plan ahead for best location for our distribution centers based on demand distribution, storage and transportation costs!
